### About The Company

Changers is a company that is dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations to adopt healthy and sustainable lifestyles, while also protecting the environment. By combining corporate wellness promotion, employee motivation, and environmental protection, Changers offers a unique set of services that are making a real difference on a global scale. 

### Responsibilities

As a front-end developer at changers.com, I was responsible for maintaining the company's front-end administration side application and developing a mobile hybrid app using ReactJs. The app served as an SDK for users to extend or include it within their own apps, allowing their users to access Changers' services of environmental protection.

### Tools, Tech & Skills

The tools used at the time were: 
- Javascript
- ReactJS
- Redux
- Storybook
- Styled System

Some of the skills I learnt and/or flexed during this time were:
- Responsive Web Design
- Third party APIs integrations
- SDK development and WebView Integration with ReactJs
- Redux architecture and Context API
- Component prototyping and delivery with Storybook
