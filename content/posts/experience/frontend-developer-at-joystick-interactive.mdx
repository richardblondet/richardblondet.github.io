### About The Company

Joysitick Interactive, now known as Incube Joystick, was a digital markeing agency based in New York with offices in LA and London, Sydney and (at the time of joining) Santo Domingo. Among their portfolio of clients were Disney, LG, Scotts and WebMD.

### Responsibilities

My responsibilities were, but not limited to implementing the designs into fully HTML + Javascript rich media ads for their clients and variety of ad campaigns, to be used in Google DoubleClick Studio and other ad placement service tools.

### Tools, Tech & Skills

The tools used at the time were: 
- Javascript
- HTML + CSS
- Other tools included Photoshop to extract assets from design files
- Sublime Text 2 as the main text editor

Some of the skills I learnt and/or flexed during this time were:
- Responsive Web Design
- Third party APIs integrations
- DoubleClick Studio and Studio Certification
- Lightbox Ads
- Mobile Rich Media
- Rich Media Gallery
